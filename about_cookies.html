All About Cookies

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your computer or mobile device by your web browser. They are used to remember information about you, such as your login details or preferences, over a period of time.

Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are erased when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.
  • First-party Cookies: These are set by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party Cookies: These are set by a third party, such as an advertiser, through the website you are visiting.

Why Are Cookies Used?

Cookies serve various purposes such as:

  • Remembering login credentials
  • Keeping track of items in shopping carts
  • Personalizing content
  • Tracking website usage for analytics

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies if you prefer. However, disabling cookies might affect your browsing experience.
